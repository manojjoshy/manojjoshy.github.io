Visual Design for Compliance
Compendium’s CC Suite

Role: UX designer, UI designer
Date: Nov 2018- Aug 2019
Team: Jonathan Jacob, Saravanan, Manoj Joshi, Ramesh K
Tools: Photoshop, Illustrator, Trello, Skype, Moqups

Overview

Compliance Compendium, a leader in data protection and regulation, engaged Akoni Software Services to create the visual identity for their flagship product, CC Suite. Designed for small and medium enterprises (SMEs), CC Suite is intended to be a comprehensive data protection and regulation training solution. This case study explores the foundational visual design work completed before the project's suspension and the subsequent impact on the team and the product.

Project Objectives

The primary aim of this project was to create a visually compelling and functional design for CC Suite. The objectives were threefold:

Establish a Cohesive Visual
Identity

Develop a unified visual theme that aligns with the product’s purpose and appeals to the target business-to-business (B2B) market.

Enhance User Experience
(UX)

Ensure that the visual design facilitates an intuitive and engaging user experience, crucial for adoption and efficiency.

Ensure Brand Alignment

The design must reflect Compliance Compendium’s brand values and ethos, promoting trust and professionalism.

Understanding the Product

CC Suite is composed of two main categories of applications: compliance apps and productivity apps. The compliance apps are designed to assist SMEs in managing data protection and regulatory requirements effectively. These include tools for Subject Access Request Management, Data Protection Officer as a Service, Breach Management, and other critical compliance functions. On the other hand, the productivity apps focus on enhancing operational efficiency and collaboration. These include features like File Folder Management, Social Media Manager, Mail, Surveys, and Business Mail. Together, these app types provide a comprehensive suite that supports both regulatory adherence and day-to-day business productivity​.

Design Process

The design process for CC Suite involved creating a cohesive visual identity that balanced functionality and aesthetics. It began with defining the color schemes, typography, and iconography to ensure readability, brand alignment, and visual appeal.

Color Scheme

The color scheme for CC Suite was carefully crafted to ensure readability and align with industry standards. Several preset themes were developed, incorporating professional and appealing colors such as deep blues and teals. These themes provided visual coherence and were adaptable to different user preferences. Additionally, users were given guidelines to create their own themes, promoting flexibility while maintaining the product's professional aesthetic​.

Typography

The typography for CC Suite was centered around the use of Roboto, chosen for its modern, clean, and highly readable qualities. A clear hierarchy was established to enhance readability and usability across the suite. By combining Roboto’s bold and regular styles, the typography ensured easy navigation and effective information presentation, aligning with the professional and straightforward nature of the product​.

Iconography

Custom icons were meticulously designed for CC Suite to represent the functionalities of both compliance and productivity apps. Each icon was tailored to its specific purpose, ensuring intuitive recognition and differentiation among app features. The icons maintained a consistent style across the suite, contributing to a cohesive and professional visual identity while enhancing usability through clear and meaningful representations​.

Layout & Navigation

The layout creation for CC Suite focused on an intuitive and user-friendly structure. The navigation was designed to be straightforward, with clear access to key features and tools. A logical flow of information was established, ensuring users could easily move between tasks. The main area of each screen was organized to prioritize essential functions, minimizing clutter while maintaining an efficient workspace for users, making the entire suite easy to navigate and use​.
